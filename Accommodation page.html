Accommodation for students in Aberystwyth

Finding a suitable accommodation is an essential thing for every student. This will be your new home for at least year, so you have to feel comfortable in this place. Aberystwyth students, regardless from which year, have a wide access to university residences. Bills, wifi and proximity of Uni buildings are warranted. Prices starts from 86f per week. It is a great deal if you are interested in doing sports, because being a resident of one of university accommodation, you have premium membership to Campus Sport Centre.

Another option is to find accommodation in private sector. Aberystwyth as a typical student town has quite a big and developed market of properties. You have various option: from renting all apartament for you, by studios to single rooms. If you already have some friend, you can look for your future accommodation together: property agencies often offer all houses (usually bills included) with 5-8 rooms for groups as yours. But if you don't have potential flatmates yet, don't worry - because of every year rotation, house residents often look for another flatmate to one free room. There is a plenty of groups like "Aberystyth Renting Houses, "Housing Aberystwyth" on the Facebook. If you are not British, you can always look for special Facebook groups with people only from your country who live or are moving to Aberystwyth. This is also an amazing opportunity to make new friends! If you want to avoid writing to random people, you can always just text to one of property agencies in Aberystwyth. Prices are usually from 60f per week for single room in shared house.
