About Student Needs

Going to university for the first time seems like a daunting prospect to many. You have so many options for studying, eating, healthcare and social activities it can be overwhelming when you need to make a choice. Also, the growth of social media has meant it can be hard to find out which services current students reccommend and which they shun. This is why Student Needs was formed - to give new students advice from current Aberystwyth University students

This student-led website aims to have the answers to all questions about everything university life. We gather all informaion presented from students, via social media, emails and personal experience. In addition, everyone working at Student Needs are currently students, so you can be sure that all advice provided is relevant to all current students. From finding the best dentist in town to best accommodation that matches your needs, all you ever need to guarantee a stress-free life student life is right here.
