About Student Needs

Going to university for the first time seems like a daunting prospect to many. You have so many options for studying, eating, healthcare and social activities, and it can be hard to find out what services other students reccommend. This is why Student Needs was formed - to give new students advice from those who have already gone to Aberystwyth University, graduated and got the t-shirt.

This student-led website aims to have the answers to all questions about everything university life. From finding the best dentist in town to best accommodation that matches your needs. Advice and questions are asked and given by current students. All you ever need to guarantee a stress-free life student life is right here.
